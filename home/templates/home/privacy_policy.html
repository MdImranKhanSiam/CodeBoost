01

Information We Collect

A. From Google Login

When you authenticate via Google OAuth, we receive:

  • Your Google display name
  • Your email address (used as your account identifier)
  • Your Google profile picture (used as your default avatar)
  • A unique Google account identifier

B. Profile Information You Provide

Through the Edit Profile panel, you may optionally add:

  • A custom display name and bio
  • A profile photo uploaded from your device
  • Gender, date of birth, and country
  • Personal website URL, social media link, and GitHub link
  • Preferred programming language

All of this information is optional and entirely under your control. You can update or remove it at any time.

C. Activity Data

  • Code submissions and their automated judging results
  • Contest participation history and ratings
  • Problem-solving activity (used to generate your heatmap)
  • Session data (login timestamps, browser type)

D. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Phone numbers or physical addresses
  • Payment information (CodeBoost is currently free)
  • Precise geolocation data
  • Any data from your contacts, microphone, or camera
02

Profile Photos & Image Uploads

When you upload a custom profile photo, that image is:

  • Transmitted securely to our cloud media storage provider
  • Stored under your account and displayed publicly on your profile page
  • Accessible by anyone who views your CodeBoost profile

Cloud Storage Provider: Profile images are stored on Cloudinary. By uploading a photo, you also agree to be subject to Cloudinary's data handling practices. See cloudinary.com/privacy.

We retain your uploaded avatar as long as your account is active. If you upload a new photo, the previous one is replaced. If you delete your account, your uploaded images will be removed from storage.

You are solely responsible for the content of images you upload. Do not upload images that are offensive, illegal, or infringe on the rights of others. We reserve the right to remove any inappropriate images without notice.

03

Social Media Links & External URLs

You may optionally add external links to your public profile, including:

  • Personal website — any URL you choose
  • Social media link — e.g. Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn
  • GitHub profile — link to your GitHub account

These links are publicly visible on your profile. We do not have any relationship with the social platforms you link to and are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

We store these URLs as plain text in your profile record. We do not follow, scrape, or access the linked pages. You can remove or change these links at any time through the Edit Profile panel.

04

How We Use Your Information

We use collected data exclusively to operate and improve CodeBoost:

  • Creating and managing your user account
  • Displaying your profile name, avatar, and public profile information
  • Evaluating code submissions and calculating scores/ratings
  • Maintaining contest leaderboards and activity heatmaps
  • Sending important account-related emails (e.g. security notices)
  • Detecting and preventing abuse, cheating, or policy violations
  • Improving platform performance and reliability

No marketing. We do not use your data to send promotional emails or target you with advertisements. CodeBoost products are ad-free.

03

User Content & Submissions

Your code submissions are stored on our servers to:

  • Evaluate correctness against test cases
  • Display your submission history on your profile
  • Track progress across problems and contests

You own your code. We do not claim ownership over any code you write and submit to CodeBoost.

06

Cookies & Tracking

CodeBoost uses minimal, essential cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in during your session (session cookie)
  • Protect against CSRF attacks (Django CSRF token)
  • Remember basic preferences

We do not use third-party advertising cookies, tracking pixels, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any behavioural analytics tools.

07

Data Sharing & Third-Party Providers

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party, ever.

We work with the following service providers who may process your data as part of normal platform operation:

Google (OAuth) — handles authentication. Google Privacy Policy
Cloudinary — stores your uploaded profile photos. Cloudinary Privacy Policy
Hosting & Database provider — hosts the platform infrastructure under strict confidentiality.

We may also share data if required by law, court order, or to protect platform security.

08

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including:

  • Secure Google OAuth authentication (no passwords stored by us)
  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • CSRF protection on all forms
  • Server-side access controls and rate limiting (django-axes)
  • Secure, HttpOnly session cookies
  • Environment-based secrets management (no hardcoded credentials)

No system is 100% secure. We encourage you to protect your Google account with a strong password and two-factor authentication.

07

Children's Privacy

CodeBoost is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 13.

If we become aware that a user under 13 has created an account, we will promptly delete their data and close the account. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has registered, please contact us immediately.

10

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide platform functionality. Upon account deletion:

  • Your profile data and social links are removed from our database
  • Your uploaded avatar is deleted from Cloudinary storage
  • Your submission history is removed or anonymized

Anonymized, aggregated data (e.g. platform-wide statistics) may be retained for analysis without personal identifiers.

11

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access your profile and submission data
Update or correct your profile at any time
Replace or remove your uploaded avatar
Remove social media links from your profile
Request full account deletion
Request a copy of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@codeboost.com.

10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we may notify you via the platform or email.

Continued use of CodeBoost after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

Privacy concerns? Contact us at support@codeboost.com — we respond within 48 hours.
